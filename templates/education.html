Technical Analysis Basics
Common Technical Indicators

The RSI is a momentum indicator that measures the magnitude of recent price changes to evaluate overbought or oversold conditions. Values above 70 typically indicate overbought conditions, while values below 30 suggest oversold conditions.

MACD is a trend-following momentum indicator that shows the relationship between two moving averages of a security's price. The MACD line crossing above the signal line is considered a bullish signal, while crossing below is bearish.
